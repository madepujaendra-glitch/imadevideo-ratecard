How It Works

Terms &
Process

01
Clear Brief is Required

A complete and detailed brief is required before production begins — references, mood, tone, target audience. A vague brief leads to missed expectations. Revisions caused by an unclear brief do not count as free revisions.

02
50% DP Before Production

A down payment of 50% of the total price is required before production starts. No project will begin without payment confirmation. The remaining 50% is due once the project is completed and ready for delivery.

03
Revision Limit

Revisions are limited to the number included in your chosen package. Extra revisions beyond the limit will incur an additional charge based on complexity. Revisions apply to minor changes only — not full concept overhauls.

04
Invoice & Payment

An invoice will be sent automatically for every transaction. Payment is via PayPal. Please keep all payment receipts for your records and as mutual proof of transaction.

05
File Delivery

Final edited files are delivered in full-resolution MP4 format optimized for the requested platform (Shorts / Reels / YouTube). Files are sent via Google Drive or WeTransfer.

06
Revision Charge Notice

If revisions exceed the agreed limit, additional charges will be communicated and must be approved before work continues. There are no hidden fees — everything is discussed transparently upfront.
